Beyoncé

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter (/biˈɒnseɪ/ i bee-ON-say;[4] born September 4, 1981)[5] is an American singer, songwriter, and businesswoman. Known as "Queen Bey", she has been widely recognized for her boundary-pushing artistry, vocals, and performances. Her contributions to music and visual media and her concert perfomances have led her to become a prominent cultural figure of the 21st century. She was named the eighth-greatest singer of all time by Rolling Stone in 2023.[6]
